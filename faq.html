Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Oak & Eagle?

Oak & Eagle LLC is a land buyer in North Carolina & surrounding states. We buy, sell, and list land in all different shapes & sizes.

Does O&E buy anywhere?

Absolutely! Although North Carolina is our favorite state, we'll buy just about anywhere that makes sense. Our offers are always completely transparent with the breakdown behind the math out in plain view.

Does the land have to be cleared?

Nope! The land can have trees or properties on it, we look at every possible exit strategy before making you an offer to ensure we're giving you the highest amount possible.

What if I owe money on the land?

Doesn't matter. We can work around the bank, do land contracts, or completely pay off any liens prior to ownership transfers. We're creative!

How long does the process take?

We can close in as little as 7 days! The timeline depends on your needs - we can work quickly if you need to sell fast, or take more time if you need flexibility.

Are there any fees or commissions?

No! We cover all closing costs and there are no realtor fees or commissions. You receive the full agreed-upon amount with no surprise deductions.

Do I need to make repairs or clean up the property?

Not at all! We buy land in any condition. Whether it's raw land, has structures, or needs cleanup, we'll make you a fair offer as-is.

How do you determine the offer amount?

We analyze market data, location, size, zoning, and potential uses for the land. Our offers are data-driven and transparent - we'll show you exactly how we arrived at the number.

What types of land do you buy?

We buy all types of vacant land: residential lots, rural land, unimproved parcels, inherited land, tax-burdened properties, and land without road access or utilities. If it's vacant land, we're interested!

Is there any obligation after I submit my property information?

No obligation at all! You can review our offer and decide if it works for you. There's no pressure - we want you to feel comfortable with the process.
